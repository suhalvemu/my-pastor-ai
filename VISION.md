# my-pastor-ai — Vision & Product Plan

## Why This Exists

Most Christians read the Bible but struggle to understand what a passage truly means — its historical context, its original language nuance, its theological depth. Even more challenging is bridging that understanding to real life: *"What does this verse mean for what I am going through right now?"*

Existing Bible apps are great at delivering Scripture but poor at helping you understand and apply it personally. They answer "what does this verse mean?" but not "what does this mean **for me, today, in this situation**?"

**my-pastor-ai** is built to fill that gap. It is a wise, knowledgeable AI pastor available 24/7 — one who deeply understands Scripture and listens to your specific situation to offer guidance, comfort, and clarity grounded in the Word of God.

This is not a product. This is a service to God. It is open source, free, and built for every Christian globally.

---

## The Core Experience

The user opens the app and simply describes what they are going through:

> *"I have to make a big career decision and I am afraid of making the wrong choice. What does the Bible say about this?"*

> *"My friend is going through a divorce and is broken. I want to share something from Scripture that will comfort them."*

> *"I just read John 15:5 but I don't understand what 'abide in me' really means in practice."*

The AI Pastor responds with:
- **Deep explanation** — meaning of the passage, historical/cultural context, original language insight
- **Personal application** — guidance specific to the situation the user described
- **Relevant Scripture** — additional verses that speak to the situation
- **Encouragement** — warm, pastoral tone that feels like a trusted guide, not a search engine

---

## Target Users

- Christians globally, across all denominations
- People who read the Bible but struggle to understand or apply it
- People facing life decisions, struggles, grief, confusion, joy — who want biblical guidance
- People who want to help someone else (friend, family) with a Scripture-based response
- No age restriction — designed to be accessible to all

## Core Features

### Phase 1 — Web App (MVP)

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Bible Reader** | Read the Bible by book, chapter, verse. Starting with NIV translation. |
| **AI Pastor Chat** | Describe your situation or ask about any verse — get deep, personalized biblical guidance. |
| **Verse Explainer** | Select any verse while reading → instantly get explanation + life application. |
| **Conversational Context** | User describes their current situation in their own words — no fixed profile or categories. AI responds to exactly what the user is going through right now. |

### Phase 2 — Mobile App
- All Phase 1 features
- Push notifications for daily verse
- Offline Bible reading

### Phase 3 — WhatsApp Bot
- Ask your AI Pastor via WhatsApp
- Share a verse or situation via message
- Receive pastoral guidance instantly

## AI Pastor Personality

- Warm, compassionate, and personal — like a pastor who genuinely cares about you
- Theologically grounded — responses always rooted in Scripture
- Non-denominational — respects all Christian traditions
- Never judgmental — meets the user where they are
- Honest — does not give empty comfort; gives truth with love

---

## What This Is Not

- Not a replacement for a real pastor or church community
- Not a monetized product — free forever, no ads, no subscriptions
- Not a generic chatbot — every response is grounded in Scripture
- Not restricted to any denomination, region, or language (over time)
